No disk, RAM only:

qemu-system-aarch64 -machine virt -cpu cortex-a72 -m 2048 -nographic -kernel kernel.elf


With disk:

qemu-system-aarch64 -machine virt -cpu cortex-a72 -m 2048 -nographic -kernel kernel.elf -drive if=none,file=test_disk.img,format=raw,id=virtio-disk0 -device virtio-blk-device,drive=virtio-disk0
